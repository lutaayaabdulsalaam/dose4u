About & methodology
Our mission

Dose4U was built to simplify safe, evidence-based medicine dosing — prioritizing children, caregivers, and community health workers in Uganda. We bridge the gap between complex clinical guidelines and everyday practice by combining weight, age, and local formularies into actionable recommendations.

How it works
  • 1️⃣
    Enter patient details

    Name, weight (kg), age — simple inputs, no clutter. Designed for fast clinical workflow.

  • 2️⃣
    AI + clinical rules engine

    Our logic integrates Ugandan National Medical Stores guidelines, WHO EMLc, and pediatric dosing references to calculate safe, weight-adjusted doses.

  • 3️⃣
    Clear recommendation

    You receive a plain‑language prescription: medicine form (tablet/liquid), exact dose, frequency, and safety warnings — all in seconds.

How we help

For parents & caregivers — Quick, understandable dose guidance for common childhood conditions: fever, malaria, respiratory infections, diarrhoea.

For community health workers — A reliable second check before dispensing, aligned with national formularies (Coartem®, amoxicillin, ORS, zinc, etc).

Uganda‑specific context — Medicines reflect Uganda National Medical Stores (NMS) and Essential Medicines and Health Supplies List. We prioritise pediatric formulations (syrups, suspensions, dispersible tablets).

Clinical decision support — not a substitute

Dose4U is an informational tool designed to assist healthcare workers and caregivers. It does NOT replace professional clinical judgment. Always verify with a pharmacist, paediatrician, or prescribing clinician — especially for neonates, critically ill patients, or complex comorbidities. In case of emergency, seek immediate medical attention.
